Infobase

Stonegate // 2014 - 2018

This was a project with a small beginning but a promise to become something much greater as time went on. Initially capable of simple office manangement tasks such as booking meeting rooms and holidays, it eventually spread its roots into analysing production data, managing IT developments and even mapping out affected areas of avian disease outbreaks on a map of the UK.

Infobase was used across the company, which meant that the user system needed to be flexible. Departments should only have access to certain modules, and each user would have certain capabilities within those modules.

Farm Management System

Stonegate // 2014 - 2018

The Farm Management System was a tool for Stonegates egg producers, allowing them to record, manage and analyse everything about their flocks life. Their production data was recorded every week, and returned to them in the form of helpful graphs and analytics. They were alerted to key events in their flocks life and how to handle them. Common proccesses between Stonegate and their producers could be automated. It also served as a central line of communication, so producers could be notified of news and events.

The main challenge in this project was keeping it suitable to its audience. Many of Stonegates producers were less computer literate than the average person, and so extra care had to be taken to make sure the system was constantly clear and intuitive.
