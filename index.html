Infobase

Stonegate // 2014 - 2018

This was a project with a small beginning but a promise to become something much greater as time went on. Initially capable of simple office manangement tasks such as booking meeting rooms and holidays, it eventually spread its roots into analysing production data, managing IT developments and even mapping out affected areas of avian disease outbreaks on a map of the UK.

Modularity

This potential was made clear to me from the start, so the system had to be designed with its future expectations in mind. This meant making the user and security systems flexible. One module could have different levels of permissions to another, yet all those permissions needed to be managed easily from one space. Each user would have a list of modules they had access to, and then varying levels of capabilities within that access. All of this could be managed from an admin panel.
